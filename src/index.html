Here at Chilli + Garlic we have a simple mission: to get as many people cooking and loving their food as we possibly can. When we first started the site, we never imagined where this journey would take us, yet we remain as passionate about food today as we were when cooking in our tiny home kitchen five short years ago.

We want you to share that passion. We want you to cook more of what you want, how you want it, when you want it. If you’re going to eat three times a day, you may as well eat food you love, right? So strap on your apron, roll up your sleeves and get cooking!
